Bengaluru Runners Jatre

In the past half a decade, Bengaluru has become the running capital of India - Thanks to Bengaluru's weather, countless parks, its numerous lakes and a favourable altitude, Bengaluru often sees many elites come and train here and of course the population mix is such a lovely melting pot! Bengaluru has produced many national and international level athletes in the short, middle, long and ultra running distances.
We bring a first of its kind Bengaluru Runners Jatre, to you on the First Sunday of June (5th June 2022). As the name suggests, it will be a carnival of runners and to-be-runners across Bengaluru coming together to run various categories in the event.
