➢ Gear preparation

  • Running Shoes: We recommend all runners to wear comfortable shoes and complete the run. [Barefoot running is not advisable - Unless you are barefoot runner, though not recommended] Comfortable T-shirt & shorts/pants
  • Emergency contact name and number on the Bib
  • Your Bib needs to be clearly visible and can be read by onlookers, race officials/ organisers, timing solutions teams and photographers.
  • How to wear a bib
    • The best position for the bib is across your abdomen, not your chest. Careful of not putting it too low as it may be close to your legs and hinder the running motion
    • Use safety pins to secure the bib.
    • Start with one of the top corners first
    • After securing both the top corners of your bib, move to the bottom corners
    • Just double check with your running hand movement and leg movement that the placement of bib is not hindering your motion

Kids 3k event details

➢ Kids will have a similar flow and experience as adults except for

  • They will have Duplicate bibs [one for the kid and other to be worn by one of the parents
  • There will be two wristbands with the kid's bib number and name [to be worn by the kid and one of the parent]

➢ Event day t-shirt to be worn mandatorily by the kids for the safety of identifying kids on the course

➢ Bib to have the kidsâ€™ emergency contact name and number mentioned clearly

➢ Kids Holding Area will open at 5AM

  • The holding area will be a well cordoned off premise with ample volunteers, organizers and security guards
  • Parents participating in 10K / 5K events can drop off their kids here
  • Every kid running in the event will have to report with the organizers/ volunteers in the holding area
  • Each kid will be marked thrice through the course of the event
    • Entering the holding area
    • Starting the race
    • Finishing the race
    • Unless there is a verification process completed with matching bib/ wristbands with parent, the kid will remain in the holding area with the organizers post race finish
    • Volunteers will not hand over kids directly at the finish line for safety reasons
  • Port-a-potties available in the Kids area
  • Every kid running the event will be in the holding area till her/ his age category race flags off

➢ Kids will be engaged with various activities till 6:30

➢ Following that the kids will be arranged in age category starting from the eldest to youngest. There will be three age categories

  • 5-7 years
  • 8-11 years
  • 12-14 years

➢ First the 12-14 yrs age category will be warmed up, taken to the start point and their 3K race flag off will happen at at 7:30AM

➢ Post a 10 mins gap, the Second age category of kids i.e, 8-11years will flag off by 7:40AM

➢ Post a 10 mins gap, the Third [last] age category of kids i.e, 5-7years will flag off by 7:50AM

➢ Parents are highly encouraged not to run along with kids. It's for every kids safety

➢ We will have pacers to run in each kid category. Kids are free to run along with the pacers or at their own pace
