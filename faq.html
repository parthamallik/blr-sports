Frequently asked questions !


  • When is the Bengaluru Runners Jatre Event?

The event is scheduled on Sunday, 05 June 2022.

  • Where is the event being held?

The event will be held in HSR Layout, Bangalore

  • How do I register for the event?

You can register on our website  https://jatre.blrsports.org/

  • When will the registrations for the BRJ 2022 edition close?

Registrations closed for the 1st edition Bengaluru Runners Jatre on 29th May 2022, 06PM.

  • Are on-spot registrations available on event day or at Bib Distribution?

No, there will be no on-spot registrations either on the Bib Distribution day or the day of the event

  • What does the registration fee cover?
    • Complimentary Race Day T-shirt (sizes based on availability)
    • Hot Refreshments 
    • Route Support including Hydration
    • Timing Bib (for timed 10K and 5K runs)
    • Finisher Medals.
  • What if the T-shirt doesn’t fit me well? Can I exchange it?

Race Day T-shirts are complimentary. The sizes are ordered well in advance. While we try our best to get you the correct size, the organizers will not be held responsible in case of a shortage of any t-shirt size.

  • Can I register offline?

We encourage you to register online through our website jatre.blrsports.org

  • Can I change my running category once I have registered?

Please send an email to reach@blrsports.org. We will try our best to help you out.

The change in race category will be entertained only till 30th May 2022

  • There was a problem during registration. I have been charged but I did not receive a confirmation. What do I do?

Please check your SPAM folder for our confirmation email. In case you did not receive the confirmation, under exceptional cases, in case the transaction failed at Razorpay, and the money has been debited, please be rest assured that the money will be credited back to your account automatically in 3 working days. You may also email us at reach@blrsports.org with details such as the date of registration, your name and email and we will try to help you out.

  • What are the distance categories and what are the routes for each category?

Distance categories: 10K, 5K and Kids 3K [details and specifications can be found on the Event Information Page in ‘General’ section.The route maps have been updated on the Event Information Page in ‘The Race’ section. 

https://jatre.blrsports.org/event.html

  • Is there prize money ?

Yes, there is prize money for the 10K and  5K race categories. Please find the details  updated on the Event Information Page in ‘Post Race’ section. 

https://jatre.blrsports.org/event.html

  • What hydration support will be provided ?

There will be water stations at the Start and Finish.

There will be 7 aid stations enroute. 

You can also find water stations near the entrance and exit of the stadium

  • Is my registration money refundable ?

Registration money is not refundable on any account. In case you are unable to run for whatever reason, we encourage you to transfer the bib on another person’s name who is in a position to run the event. This change will be entertained only till 29th May 2022.

  • Will there be a drop-bag facility ?

There will be a baggage counter available on the day of the event.

You will be provided with baggage tags in the bag of the bib distribution. Please remember to use the tag and write your bib details very clearly.

There will be no tags distributed at the baggage counter on the event day.

—------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • For the Kids 3k, Can I accompany my kids on the route?

Parents will be allowed to cheer along the route but will not be allowed to run alongside their wards for the safety of other kids.

  • My kid is running the 3k, I am also running the 5k/10k. Who takes care of my kid when I am on the route?
    If your kid has also registered for the 3k, you can leave them in the designated kids holding area. Please note that kids have to mandatorily wear the event t-shirts and BIBs with your and / or emergency contact name and information mentioned on the reverse. 

 

  • Where can I collect my kid(s) after the run?

Kids will finish their run and stay within the holding area until they are picked up by their parents/guardians. Please go through the Kids 3K event webinar presentation deck sent out to you for further details. 

Alternatively you can head to our event information page and run down to the ‘Kids 3K event details’ for all the particulars. https://jatre.blrsports.org/event.html
 

  • Do I have to give any documents to pick back up by kid(s)?

Only the parent/guardian with the Duplicate Kid Bib and Wrist Bands with kid Bib number written on both bands will be allowed to pick up your kid(s)

  • Can non-registered friends and family buy refreshments over the counter?

The registration covers refreshments only for the participant. 

Non-registered parents/guardians may purchase food coupons on the Bib Distribution Day - 04th May 2022 for a nominal fee.

NO COUPONS WILL BE SOLD AT EVENT LOCATION. 

  • Will there be prizes?

Kids event will have podiums across 3 age categories: 5 years to 7 years. 8 years to 11 years and 11 years to 14 years. There will not be any prize money for the kids.
