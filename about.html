Bengaluru Runners Jatre

We bring a first of its kind ‘Bengaluru Runners Jatre’, to you on the First Sunday of June | 5th June 2022. As the name suggests, it will be a carnival of runners and to-be-runners across Bengaluru coming together to run various categories in the event.
Bengaluru Runners Jatre is an event brought together by the running fraternity joining hands with RWAs, BBMP, local leaders, Police, NGOs and the community at large. Various stakeholders are coming together to make running accessible to all.
From an execution view point, the running fraternity have come together and set up the Bengaluru Sports Foundation Trust; a Not for Profit Organisation; which will host the event.
We believe that ‘Anyone can run’, and that is exactly what we are promoting here where we will have kids, adolescents, adults, senior citizens, people with physical disabilities, working mothers, busy IT professionals, entrepreneurs, home makers, and anyone you can think of running in our event.

Vision

We envision that the already popular running hub of India- Bengaluru, also becomes a city where running is the first and go-to choice of fitness for everyone. We visualise running to become everyone's lifestyle - age no bar, gender no bar, occupation / profession no bar.
We are viewing this event as an opportunity to have as many local institutions like schools, colleges, hospitals, diagnostic centres, fitness facilities, NGOs, local businesses and more to be engaged and promoted in various capacities. The point of view is to have everyone come out and support this event as a festival of running. We would love for people to be excited and wait for this event every year and proactively own up and contribute in ways they can.

Why ‘Runners’ Jatre?’

Firstly we believe ‘Anyone can Run’, and secondly we want to stir the entire city's sentiments to venture on the streets of Bengaluru - either to run or to support the run.
As an organization, we will invest our time in educating about running, its myths, benefits, by professional and veteran runners, bring in awareness about any running specific gears for the enthusiasts, have panel discussions amongst different players in the running fraternity explaining why and how will running can become a lifestyle change and not just a fad sport
We see this whole event coming together as a huge carnival but specific to running as a form of fitness
